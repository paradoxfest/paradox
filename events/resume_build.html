Competition Guidelines

  • The scenario (job posting or description) will be revealed at the start of the competition.
  • Individual participation only (no team entries).
  • Participants must create a résumé tailored to the job description.
  • Participants have 45 minutes to craft and submit their résumés after the scenario is revealed.
  • All résumés must be submitted through the designated online platform or email within the time limit.
  • Résumés submitted after the deadline will be automatically disqualified.
  • The judges' decision will be final.
