Competition Guidelines

The following guidelines apply to the competition:

  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • Task: Create effective prompts that maximize desired responses within specific constraints.
  • Tools: Use only approved software for prompt creation. External assistance like AI-based auto-completion or additional tools is prohibited.
  • Evaluation Criteria:
    • Effectiveness: How well does the prompt elicit the intended response?
    • Creativity: How unique and innovative is the prompt?
    • Clarity: The prompt should be concise and easy to understand.
  • Participants: A maximum of 25 participants can compete.
  • Scoring: Based on prompt performance, creativity, and adherence to the task.

Note: Each participant will be assigned a computer.
