Event Details


PROMPT ENGINEERING

Guidelines

  • 1 Duration: 30 minutes.
  • 2 Task: Create effective prompts that maximize desired responses within specific constraints.
  • 3 Tools: Use only approved software for prompt creation. External assistance like AI-based auto-completion or additional tools is prohibited.
  • 4 Evaluation Criteria:
    • – Effectiveness: How well does the prompt elicit the intended response?
    • – Creativity: How unique and innovative is the prompt?
    • – Clarity: The prompt should be concise and easy to understand.
  • 5 Participants: A maximum of 25 participants can compete.
  • 6 Scoring: Based on prompt performance, creativity, and adherence to the task.
  • Note: Each participant will be assigned a computer.
