Competition Guidelines

  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • ID Requirement: All participants must bring their college ID card
  • Computer Assignment: Each participant will be assigned a computer or laptop with the typing software pre-installed.
  • Content: A hardcopy of the content will be provided. Participants must type exactly as it appears, including formatting, capitalization, punctuation, and spacing.
  • Prohibited Tools: The use of external tools, including autocorrect, spell-checker, and other software is strictly prohibited during the competition.
  • Disqualification: Any participant found engaging in malpractice will be disqualified immediately.
  • Scoring: Score will be based on typing speed (WPM) and accuracy. Results will be announced after evaluating these metrics.
  • Participants: A maximum of 25 participants can compete in this event.
  • Rule Changes: Organizers have the right to make changes in rules.
