About PARADOX '24

Paradox '24 is the annual flagship technical fest organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the College of Engineering Kidangoor. It’s where technology, innovation, and creativity collide to create an electrifying experience.

This year, we’re bringing you a unique blend of hands-on workshops, interactive events, and mind-blowing tech showcases that push the boundaries of what’s possible. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a problem-solver, or simply curious, PARADOX '24 promises an unforgettable journey into the future of innovation.

Brace yourself for a thrilling ride—because at PARADOX '24, the unexpected is just the beginning!
